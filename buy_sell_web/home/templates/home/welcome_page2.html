How to exchange money using ExonTime
01
Register and Verify Email for getting access to ExonTime services
Go to registration page by clicking 'register' from the top right of the page, then do the following:
  • Complete the registration form and submit.
  • Then check your email for the verification email
  • Click the verification link from the email and then you can login to our website
02
Placing an order from Home/Exchange page
Scroll to the top of the Exchange/Home page and follow the steps below:
  • Select from where you want to send money and where you want to receive. 
  • Then select the sending amount. The receiving amount will be automatically updated according to our price. 
  • After that’s done, click the “Buy” button to place order.
  • Please, Check the limit and our reserves before selecting amount.
03
Give us your receiving account number and transaction
After placing an order you will be automatically redirected to another page for additional information which are required for completing order
  • Provide us your sending account, receiving account and the transaction id of you payment.
  • After that’s done, click the “Submit” button.
  • If your information are correct then you will receive your money within the shortest possible time.
04
If you want to sell bitcoin the follow these steps
If you placed order for selling your bitcoin then we will provide you our BTC wallet address within a web-page
  • Copy the wallet address or scan the QR code and sens btc to that address. 
  • If the payment is successful then you will get a success message and you will get your money after we receive at least on confirmation from Blockchain
05
Verify your identity and address
For increasing your limits you have to verify your identity and address by following the steps shown below:
  • Upload your proof of identity and address document from document upload page.
  • We will check those document and if its fulfils our criteria then your account limit will be increased.
